Make Login Simple

Every developer knows that easy to use applications are more likely to succeed. So why do most applications still make it hard to sign in? Simplifying your login is one of the most important upgrades you can make to your application. The industry is becoming more competitive, and companies that neglect to optimize their UX design will likely go out of business. Swoop is a powerful upgrade to your current password-based login.

Login By Simply Sending An Email

Swoop is a login service that combines mailto technology, antispam encryption and WebSockets to provide a beautiful login experience that your users will love. Login occurs in seconds and the user does not have to set up anything.

  • No apps to download
  • No special hardware needed
  • No customer opt in required

Safe And Protected

Swoop is a major security upgrade from password login. Swoop uses the 1024/2048 bit digital keys that are part of the DKIM standard to authenticate your user. This is state of the art encryption. See how it works for more details.

No More Passwords!

Good UX is about keeping it simple to use. Swoop allows users to create accounts and login with just a few taps of their finger. No annoying passwords to create and no keyboards to fumble around with. Pure bliss for the user means more business for you
